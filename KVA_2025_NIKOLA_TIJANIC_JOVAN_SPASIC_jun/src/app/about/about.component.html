About Cinemania

Welcome to CINEMANIA, where every film becomes an experience. More than just a place to watch movies, our cinema is a space where stories come to life. Whether you’re here for the latest blockbusters, timeless classics, indie gems, or family favorites we’re dedicated to delivering the ultimate moviegoing experience.

Features

  • Browse and search movies by various criteria
  • View detailed movie information
  • Make ticket reservations
  • Manage user profile
  • Track reservation history
  • Rate watched movies

Technology Stack

  • Angular 19
  • Angular Material
  • TypeScript
  • HTML/CSS
  • Axios for HTTP requests
